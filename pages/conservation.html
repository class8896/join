Wildlife Conservation — Visitor Notes

Prioritize animal welfare and habitat protection.

Respectful Wildlife Viewing

Keep distance, avoid feeding, and reduce noise.

  • Use binoculars
  • Follow signage

Habitats & Seasons

Many species have specific breeding or nesting periods.

  • Stay on marked tracks
  • Observe closures

Community & Conservation

Support accredited sanctuaries and education efforts.

  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Report issues to authorities

Leave No Trace Basics

Pack out waste and protect water sources.

  • Stay on durable surfaces
  • Minimize campfire impacts where allowed

FAQs

Can I touch or hold wildlife?

Avoid physical contact to prevent stress and harm.

Is feeding okay?

No. It can alter behaviour and diets.

Are drones allowed?

Rules vary and may restrict use near wildlife. Check local guidance.
