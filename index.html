Seasonal Highlights

Spring: Cherry Blossoms

Visit Bonn or Berlin to see the streets come alive with pink cherry blossoms (Kirschblüten) in April.

Summer: Baltic Beaches

Escape the heat on the islands of Rügen or Usedom. Perfect for cycling and sustainable stays.

Winter: Christmas Markets

Experience the magic of traditional markets in Nuremberg, Dresden, and Cologne. Look for eco-certified stalls.

Travel Fact

Neuschwanstein Castle

Germany is home to over 20,000 castles. The most famous, Neuschwanstein, served as the inspiration for Disney's Sleeping Beauty castle.
